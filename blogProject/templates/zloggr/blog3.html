Amazon Fire Tv Stick

The NO. 1 Home Entertainment Streaming Technology?

Posted by Aniekan Essiet on July 01, 2021

Amazon fire stick is the no. 1 home entertainment streaming technology of 2021. It comes with a variety of both streaming and live TV services. It also comes with the Dolby Atmos (This is a technology that creates audio, visual and voice technologies for movies, TV and music) and the major streaming services.

WHATS THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN THE AMAZON FIRE TV STICK AND THE AMAZON FIRE TV STICK 4K?

Well, just as the name applies, the only difference is the 4k resolution.

DO I HAVE TO PAY MONTHLY FOR THE AMAZON FIRE TV STICK?

The Amazon fire stick, you only need the independent streaming services tariff and nothing more. You don’t have to pay an extra tariff for the stick. For example if you already have a Netflix subscription, when you connect your TV stick to the TV you don’t need another subscription from Amazon.

DOES IT HAVE VOICE CONTROL?

As you would expect the Amazon Fire TV stick is a product from Amazon and of course has the Alexa voice control in-built.

WHAT ARE THE PRICES?

It ranges from $29 which is for the Amazon fire TV stick lite, $39 for the Amazon Fire TV stick without the 4k resolution, and you can get the Amazon Fire TV stick with the 4k resolution for around $49.

WHAT ARE THE REQUIREMENTS FOR THE TV STICK?

The fire TV stick works on any type of TV with HDMI ports (which most modern TV’s have today). And the only requirement is internet connection.

HOW TO SET UP?

The Amazon Fire TV stick can be set up in three easy steps. And it stays hidden.

  • Plug in behind the TV
  • Turn on the TV
  • Connect to the internet
