Amazon Echo Dot (3rd Gen)

Your Smart Speaker

Posted by Aniekan Essiet on July 08, 2021

The Amazon Echo Dot (3rd Gen) is a compact smart speaker with inbuilt ALEXA virtual assistant that fits perfectly into small spaces. With the Echo Dot and the ALEXA voice control you are also able to do a number of things ranging from playing music, answering questions, reading the news checking the weather, setting alarms, controlling other compatible smart devices and many more.

WHAT TYPE OF SONGS CAN I PLAY ON AMAZON ECHO DOT?

When connected to the internet you can stream from any of your preferred music providing service providers such as Amazon Prime, Spotify, Deezer, Apple music, Sirius XM, etc.

There’s actually a free tariff on Amazon prime for all Echo Dot owners.

If you prefer to play songs directly from your music library on your phone, you can connect to it through Bluetooth.

Yes, with the Amazon Echo Dot, you can make call hands-free.

IS THERE A MONTHLY FEE?

WHAT’S THE DIFFERENCE BETWEEN ECHO DOT AND ECHO?

The main difference is the compactness and portability of the Echo Dot when compared to just Echo

IS ALEXA ECHO?

Not actually, ALEXA is a voice control virtual assistant while Echo is a smart speaker with the in-built virtual assistant.
