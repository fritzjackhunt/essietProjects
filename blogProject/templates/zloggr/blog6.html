WHAT ARE FITNESS TRACKERS?

Fitness trackers are electronic devices that observe or plot the physical condition about a person whether he/she/them is in shape or good condition. They include a number of observations (which sometimes can be customized) such as steps, heart rate, sleep rate etc.
