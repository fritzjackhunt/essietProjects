HOW TO CONNECT

To connect the HUAWEI GT Watch 2, simply install the "HUAWEI Health app" on your smartphone, be it Android or IOS. Then click on "DEVICES". Then click on "ADD" and then "SMART WATCH". Finally select the "HUAWEI WATCH GT 2" or "HUAWEI GT WATCH SERIES" and select the type of GT Watch you have
