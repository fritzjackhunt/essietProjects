WHAT ARE WIRELESS HEADPHONES?

Wireless headphones are the sort of headphones that use a Bluetooth connection for connectivity. Just as the name implies, wireless – without wire. It is best for comfortability. Let’s be frank, we all hate when our wires get tangled in our backpack or pockets especially our earphones. Wireless headphone has been in the market for a while now, they come in different sizes. Some could be as small enough to just fit in your ears, some might be big enough to go over the head. They all have one thing in common, that is the requirement of Bluetooth for a connectivity, which means no wires. And of course good news.
